About The
JR Hoya Cross Country

Introduction▼

The Junior Hoya Cross Country team was created in 2007 in order to offer a youth running program for the students in the West Cobb area. Our Program is a branch of Harrison High School's cross country program, but it's open to all 5th - 8th graders interested in running. The Jr Hoya XC program relies on coaches annd parent volunteers to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Our Mission▼

The mission of the program is to introduce young atheletes to the sport of cross country, to promote a health lifestyle, and top encourage fun and sportsmanship through running. The runners will compete in 2 - mile cross country meets around the greater Atlanta Area (i. e. Acworth, Roswell, Jasper, Cartersville). We practice Monday and Thursday evenings for summer runs during June & July and for official practice from August through October at Lost Mountain Park. Family are offered on Saturday mornings near Illinois Monument off of Cheatham Hill Dr. In the spring we participate in a few distance track meets to keep in shape. We will work with others sports schedule, so give running a try!

We hope you want to run with us! Click on the registration tab to sign up and contact Kelly Duncan for more information and to receive remind texts about practice / meets.
