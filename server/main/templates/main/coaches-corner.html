Wise Words

Running 101: "Run Often, Mostly Easy, Some Moderate, A little Fast"

Great article on "Fueling Your Engine for Exercise"

For runners with less than 4 years of running experience, it is wise to eat a meal a minimum of 90 minutes before practice. If an early morning meal is not possible maybe try a light snack and late dinner after practice.

All of the coaches have commented on what a dedicated groupe of runners we have this summer. I appreciate their willingness to work hard, and the coaches will work hard to keep cross country positive, and rewarding experience.

June

The summer running program runs through Aug 9. In order to get a t-shirt, you must run the assigned number of minutes each week and turn in a weekly log of runs at the following link: Jr Hoya weekly Running Log. The plan below will serve as guide on how to break the weekly numbers into manageable segments. In addition, the form will change each week to indicate the suggested number of minutes for each day. We will start our summer program with a 4 week build up and then settle into 8 weeks of "normal" runs. You can run on any day, as long as your weekly total meets the requirements. Running rewards consistency! Consistency running = fun!
